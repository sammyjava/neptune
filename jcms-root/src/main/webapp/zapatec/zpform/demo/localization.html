Localization
This demo shows how to localize form messages for your language.
  • You can easily get form messages on your language. To do this - specify lang, langCountryCode and langEncoding for your Zapatec.Form constructor.
  • You also need to include file with message resources for your language into page. For english - zpform/lang/en.js etc.
  • In this demo first form uses english messages and second - russian.
